Our Mission

Wildcats Biotech is a student-led organization at the University of Arizona committed to advancing career exploration, preparation, and development in the biotechnology sector. We provide students with direct access to professionals from startups, regulatory agencies, consulting firms, and pharmaceutical companies to learn from their personal experiences—successes and setbacks alike.

We organize professional development events including Roche campus tours, alumni and industry panels, and resume-building workshops. Our community fosters engagement between undergraduate and graduate students and the broader biotech ecosystem through networking opportunities, LinkedIn profile sessions, and mock interviews with hiring managers.

Our regular meetings help members set and reflect on personal career goals, and we actively share curated resources and opportunities to accelerate their development. Wildcats Biotech welcomes students from across disciplines—biomedical sciences, engineering, data science, business, and more—recognizing that biotech is inherently collaborative and diverse.

Short-Term Initiatives

  • Guest speaker series featuring professionals from diverse biotech careers
  • Roche campus tours and industry site visits
  • Resume/CV development workshops and mock interviews
  • LinkedIn optimization sessions and alumni panels
  • Goal-setting meetings with structured reflection and planning
  • Centralized databases for internship and skill-building opportunities

Long-Term Vision

  • Expand student awareness of biotech career pathways: R&D, regulatory, business, startups, and consulting
  • Enhance student readiness for internships, graduate programs, and biotech employment
  • Build a lasting network between students and the regional biotech industry
  • Create an inclusive community where students explore and refine career goals aligned with their interests
